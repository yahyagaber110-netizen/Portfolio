Master's Thesis
● In Progress

A Proposed Tool for Vitality Assessment of Socio-Cultural Hubs Using Visual Temporal Analytical Approach and GIS

Developing a novel methodology for evaluating the living vitality of urban cultural spaces — integrating Geographic Information Systems with visual temporal analysis to understand how public hubs sustain meaning, memory, and social activity across time. The framework is validated through comparative case studies in Egyptian and international contexts.
